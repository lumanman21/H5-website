InfiRay # Our Story

About InfiRay Outdoor

InfiRay Outdoor focuses on the development of outdoor optoelectronic equipment, including thermal riflescope, handheld thermal monocular/ binoculars, night vision riflescope, and other outdoor optical devices. Our sensor technology leads the industry, while the others were still staying at 17μm, InfiRay Outdoor had led the market with 12μm.
InfiRay Outdoor deeply understand the demands of customers for natural observation, hunting, etc. Our products are designed to be ultra clear, ultra precision, ultra reliable, powerful, and ergonomic to ensure a better experience for our users. By virtue of excellent quality, reliability and experience, our products have been exported to more than 100 countries worldwide. InfiRay Outdoor, your trustworthy outdoor optoelectronic expert, helps you explore and discover new horizons!

Why Choose infiRay Outdoor?

Because of
the Leading Technology

InfiRay Outdoor has the most advanced sensor technology.
We have developed the world's first 8µm sensor.
When the pixel size of sensors in the industry was still 17µm, our 12µm technology
was widely used in outdoor products to achieve clearer visual imaging.

Because of
the Continuous Innovation

InfiRay Outdoor insists on innovation and holds the belief that every inventive step it takes will bring hunters a new and better hunting experience. Therefore, we innovatively developed the first optical zoom thermal product-Zoom Series, changing the situation of sole digital zoom thermal products in the outdoor field; we introduced Thermal+, daytime optics and thermal imaging fusion technology, into the outdoors, allowing users to hunt from day to night; in addition, we took the lead in applying advanced 1280 sensor technology to outdoor products, and developed RS75, bringing a new level of clear imaging quality.

Because We Never Compromise on Quality

Every product will only be delivered to the users after the measurement of more than 100 quality control procedures, from the optics materials selection to the production, we have used the highest standard to ensure the better products provided to the users.

Because We Understand Your Needs

We know that whether for field observation, hunting, etc. You want your devices to be clearer, to see farther, to have more functions, and to get more new experiences. That is why we bring the technologies of Ultra-Clear, Ultra-Precise and Ultra-Reliable. We understand and strive to better meet your demands.
