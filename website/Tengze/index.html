About Tengze Construction Machinery

Hefei Tengze Construction Machinery Co., LTD., located in Hefei International Trade City, Anhui Province, is a well-known trading company specializing in the import and export of construction machinery. The company has been adhering to the concept of integrity management, products all over the world, customers from all over the world: Europe, North America, South America, Italy, Southeast Asia, the Middle East, Australia, Arab and other countries, and we have established long-term friendly trade relations.
Partners all over the world
As a professional machinery and equipment supplier, our partners all over the world, from Europe to South America, to Italy, and even South Asia, the Middle East and other countries, to maintain long-term friendship and close cooperation.
