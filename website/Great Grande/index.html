ABOUT GREAT GRANDE

Sunmine’s refrigerator(freezer) products mainly cover five categories: door&cabinet vacuum forming machine, door&cabinet foaming line, assembly line, metal sheet forming line and mould manufacturing. It not only owns advanced large-scale digital controlling processing equipment and CAD/CAM processing system, but also owns digital controlling lathe, milling, grinding, boring, drilling, line cutting, bending, electric spark, argon-arc welding gas shielded welding and other special equipment. Its products also include foaming lines for filling building insulation panels with foaming agent, and foaming molds for auto carpet, dashboard, sun visors and other auto parts foaming processing. Sunmine’s products are widely used in the industries of home appliances, auto parts and building energy-saving panels. At the same time, the company has taken the lead in passing CE certification, IS09001 international quality management system, IS014001 environment system certification and certificate of GB/T28001-2001 occupational health and safety management system.
