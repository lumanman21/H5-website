about us

CARE AIR CARE YOU

Anhui Leking Environment Technology Co., Ltd founded in 2014, the company has specialized in manufacturing household air purification products such as the purifier, the humidifier and the dehumidifier，which are subdivided into a series of home, car, desktop, medicare and commercial use according to specific use cases.


The company currently owns a modern factory of 20000 square meters, 3 assembly lines, with a daily production capacity of more than 5,000 units. About 200 talents devote themselves to the production, sales, quality and R&D of this company. Its output value in 2021 topped 200 million yuan.


Our R&D department boasts 12 outstanding talents, including structural engineer, electrical control engineer, electrical engineer and industrial designer, who design and develop 6-10 new products annually on average.We strictly implement ISO9001:2015 quality management system.Our company's products with a series of certifications including GS, CE, FCC, ETL, RoHs, Energy star, AHAM,ensure the success of export to the global market.
