ABOUT US

Inno-Sign is one of the leading Chinese Drinkware Suppliers, one of the first Chinese companies to pass both the GRS and RCS audit, specializing in the R&D, design and manufacture of various drinkware, covering different materials including stainless steel, plastic, silicone, glass, ceramic etc. "WOOVO" is a brand owned by Inno, which products are 100% compliant with food-safe regulations from Europe/America with third-party test approval. We are dedicated to the continuous development of sustainable and environmentally friendly products and make our contribution to the global low-carbon green course.
