Anhui Daxin Vacuum Technology Co., Ltd.

Anhui Daxin Vacuum Technology Co., Ltd. is located in the national-level Hefei Economic and Technological Development Zone. It is a professional supplier in the field of vacuum and ammonia leak detection. The main products are vacuum box-type ammonia leak detection recovery system and ammonia recovery system. The technical team of the company utilizes rich experience in design and manufacturing and application feedback from customers to continuously improve the vacuum and ammonia detection process to ensure the reliability and advancement of the system. The ammonia detection system is non-standard customized and provides comprehensive solutions. It has been widely used in hydrogen energy (such as gas cylinders, bipolar plates, membrane electrodes, circulation pumps, injectors, etc.), automobiles, refrigeration, electric power, power management, heat dissipation Devices, fire extinguishers, aerospace and other fields.
