About JianHongHang

Ningguo JianHongHang Manufacturing Co., Ltd. is located at No. 7, ChuangYe North Road, Economic and Technological Development Zone, Ningguo City. It was founded in May 2008. It is mainly engaged in the R & D, manufacturing and sales of casting equipment, heat treatment equipment, casting mold and environmental protection equipment. It is the only “R & D base of wear-resistant casting production equipment“ designated by The Wear-resistant Casting Branch Of China Casting Association and The Technological Innovation Of China's Steel Wear-resistant Material Industry Director Unit Of Strategic Alliance And High-tech Enterprise of Anhui Province.
JianHongHang adheres to the innovation.It has successively obtained obtained 8 national invention patents and 42 utility models and presided over the formulation of the industry standard of "Iron Mold Coated Sand Molding Machine" and the local standard of "Metal Mold For Casting Grinding Ball", and formulated 9 enterprise standards. The leading product JianHongHang brand "ZZJX-F Iron Mold Coated Sand Casting Production Line" passed the new product appraisal in 2015, won the "Foundry Equipment Innovation Award" of China Foundry Association for three consecutive times, won the first prize of Xuancheng science and technology progress, and was recognized as "The First Set Of Major Technical Equipment In Anhui Province" and "Anhui Famous Brand Product".In 2019, the "Grinding Ball Automatic Sorting Machine" passed the appraisal. The patented technology of "material separation equipment" independently developed was adopted to realize the technical innovation of the casting production end process, which not only reduce the labor intensity of the casting ball separation and separation station and the occupational hazard of workplace factors to the field operators, but also improve the effective utilization rate of the workshop and realized cost saving .At present, the project has been recommended as the second prize of science and technology of China Machinery Industry in 2020 by China Foundry Standardization Committee.
