Product Description

Terbium scandium aluminum garnet (TSAG) crystal is an ideal magneto-optical material, suitable for visible and infrared wavelengths (wavelength range: 400-1600nm). TSAG crystal has the advantages of high stability, good thermal and mechanical properties, which is essential for the next generation of high-power lasers. Compared with TGG crystal, TSAG has higher feld constant and lower absorption loss, which makes the isolator more compact and is an ideal material for high power optical isolator. Main Advantages: Verdet constant 20% higher than TGG (48radT-1m-1 @ 1064nm) Low Absorption(3000ppm/cm @ 1064nm) High power laser Application Low thermally-induced birefringence Make isolator more compact and smaller Typical applications: Faraday rotator optical isolator
