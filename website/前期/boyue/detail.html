Multi-station vacuum thermoforming machine for refrigerator cabinet liner

This machine is mainly used for refrigerator cabinet vacuum thermoforming. It consists of loading station, preheating station, heating station, forming station, unloading station, etc. We also supply cutting station, punching station and option. According to the foaming craft, the line can be available: suction type and bubble type. The line adopts intelligent temperature controller, servo motor drive, low-temperature air cooler and so on. High automaticity, easy operation, high efficiency, etc.
