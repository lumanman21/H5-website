Anhui Crystro Crystal Materials Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in research and development, design, processing and sales of functional crystal materials and their application devices. Functional crystal materials mainly include: Magneto-optical crystals Terbium Gallium Garnet TGG, TSAG; Piezoelectric crystals Lithium Tantalate LiTaO3, Lithium Niobate LiNbO3, Lanthanum Gallium Silicate LGS; Scintillation crystals Ce: LuAG, Ce: GAGG; Laser crystal Nd: YAG and Single crystal epitaxial substrate crystals like GGG, SGGG, Lanthanum Aluminate LaAlO3, etc. Application devices such as electro-optical Q switch, optical isolator, Faraday rotator, etc. Optical isolator including Faraday free space isolator and fiber isolator varying from wavelength 450nm to 1100nm. Crystro, with a professional and experienced R & D, design, production, processing, and assembly team. As of today, has successfully developed 3-inch, 4-inch TGG crystals, and 4-inch, 6-inch LT and LN crystals. Also, the company is well equipped with a number of manufacturing and assembly lines which cover from crystal growth, cutting, grinding, polishing, coating to device assembly and final inspection. This allows Crystro has production capacity to satisfy massive demand of TGG, LT and LN wafer products. With Quality first mindset, Crystro has established a strict quality control and testing process and system to ensure crystal products with reliable quality. Besides ISO9001, ISO14001 and ISO 45001 certs. All products have been sent to SGS regularly for testing to ensure those meeting RoHS and REACH requirements all the time. Adhering to the commitment to the concept of providing customers with quality products and services, Crystro has established a widely range sales network all over the world, and products are welcomed by both domestic and oversea customers including customers of the United States, Germany, Japan, South Korea and other countries, and hence the company has built a long-term cooperative relationship with all of them.
