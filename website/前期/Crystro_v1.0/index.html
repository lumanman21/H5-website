Anhui Crystro Crystal Materials Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in R&D, design, processing and sales of crystal materials and their application devices. Since its establishment, the company has won the honorary titles of "National High-tech Enterprise" and "Hefei High-growth Seed Enterprise". The company's main products are functional crystalline materials and their application devices. Crystal Growth

Since the establishment of the company, it has been continuously committed to the research and development of advanced crystal materials and application products, and has achieved fruitful results. The company has successfully developed 3-inch, 4-inch TGG crystals, 4-inch, 6-inch and 8-inch LT and LN crystals, etc.

Production

In terms of production and processing, the company has equipped a number of complete production, processing and assembly lines to achieve full coverage from crystal growth, cutting, spheronization, grinding, polishing, coating to device assembly and testing. At present, the company has more than 50 crystal growth furnaces and a number of imported wafer processing lines, and has the capacity for mass production, processing and supply of TGG, LT and LN wafer products.
