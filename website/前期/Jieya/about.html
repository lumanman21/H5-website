The most trusted wet wipes manufacturer
Tongling Jieya Biologic Technology Stock Co., Ltd. also known as Joyalways,established in 1999, is an OEM specialist and a China-based enterprise engaged in wet wipe manufacturing in Asia. It was successfully listed on the Shenzhen Stock Exchange Growth Enterprise Market on December 3, 2021, achieving A-share listing. It is the first wet wipe enterprise listed on the domestic GEM. After over 20 years of development, we have already grown into a global company with exports to over 45 countries and regions and an annual wet wipes output capability of up to 1 billion.Our services: contract manufacturing and private label manufacturing of wet wipes, dry wipes,facial Masks and Cosmetic products. At present, we have two integrated factories located in Tongling, China, with over 50 production lines and 700 professional employees. We integrate research and development, design, as well as manufacturing into a whole and have obtained various international quality certifications. Joyalways has taken a lead in OEM/ODM wet wipes and has been favored by many brand partners, with its over 20 years of experience, complete management system, FDA medical-grade equipment, class 100k cleanroom workshops, along with excellent services. The most comprehensive range of converting capabilities makes us a true one-stop provider of all kinds of wet wipes while the determination towards environmental protection makes us a true believer in eco-friendly practices. When it comes to wet wipes, Tongling Jieya(Joyalways) is your best choice!
