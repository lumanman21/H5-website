ABOUT US

Hefei Youvi Trade Co., Ltd.
Born in 2015, Youvi Trade is a professional nail art company and always adhere to the principle of “HONESTY,QUALITY and EFFICIENCY". We dedicate to Nail Art Wholesale for more than ten years, we also provide samples order and trail order to meet different requests from customers, we'd like to provide solutions to our customers to help them to be more competitive in the market. Our main products are more than 500 items, including nail lamp, nail drill bits, nail dust collector, nail drill machine, nail sticker, nail decoration and other nail art tools, which have been exported to Europe, the Middle East, North America, Russia, Africa and other countries and regions. All the products have been highly recognized and praised by all our clients. Products can be designed according to special requirements from customers. OEM orders are always welcomed. If you have any questions, please contact our service support email: admin@yvnail.com.
