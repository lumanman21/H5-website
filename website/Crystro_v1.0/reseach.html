Crystal Growth

Crystro, with a professional and experienced R & D, design, production, processing, and assembly team. As of today, has successfully developed 3-inch, 4-inch TGG crystals, and 4-inch, 6-inch LT and LN crystals. Also, the company is well equipped with a number of manufacturing and assembly lines which cover from crystal growth, cutting, grinding, polishing, coating to device assembly and final inspection. This allows Crystro has production capacity to satisfy massive demand of TGG, LT and LN wafer products.

Production Equipment

In terms of production and processing, the company has equipped a number of complete production, processing and assembly lines to achieve full coverage from crystal growth, cutting, spheronization, grinding, polishing, coating to device assembly and testing. At present, the company has more than 50 crystal growth furnaces and a number of imported wafer processing lines, and has the capacity for mass production, processing and supply of TGG, LT and LN wafer products.
