Company Profile

Chuzhou Star Gifts Trading Co,Ltd is a manufacturer and exporter of bags. our main products are cooler bags,lunch bag,tote bags, shopping bags,school bags, drawstring bags, travel bags, documents bags, handbags,laptop bags, cosmetic bags and garment cover etc. we provide OEM and ODM service according to your design, such as structure,size, color, material, print and logo. our well- equipped facilities and excellent quality control throughout all stages of production enable us to guarantee total customer satisfaction.
