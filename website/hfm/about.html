Anhui Aoxuan Machinery Equipment Co., Ltd.
Anhui Aoxuan Machinery Equipment Co., Ltd. Is located in Maanshan city, Anhui province, specializing in CNC sheet metal bending, shear and other equipment research and development, production, sales,service and integration of the industry and trade company.

Since 2001,the company has a group of scientific and technological talents engaged in mechatronics, automatic control and design.After years of unremitting efforts, continuous development with science and technology combined with the practice.

The company mainly produces products are:

Hydraulic Press Brake, Hydraulic Shearing Machine, Laser Cutting Machine, Plasma Cutting Machine...
