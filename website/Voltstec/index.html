BUSINESS

Voltstec is a brand under Volts, focusing on the supply of Land Rover, Mercedes-Benz and BMW parts.

We started business in Newcastle of UK since 2008, the business moved to Shanghai of China in 2010.  Our facility has received the ISO9001:2015 certification & manufacturers have IATF16969 certifications, and have established and applies a quality management system for design, manufacture and sale into the automotive aftermarket. Manufactured to OE specification to ensure optimum performance and longevity . Voltstec has won the approval of all customers through its no compromise attitude to quality and service. Whether your need is low volume or high volume, Voltstec will work with you to meet your specific needs.
