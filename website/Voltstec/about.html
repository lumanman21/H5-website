Company Profile

Hefei Panri Construction Machinery Trading Co., Ltd. is located in the beautiful city of Hefei, the capital of Anhui Province. It is also the birthplace of the first excavator in China (the Hekuang brand series excavators produced by the original Hefei Mining Machinery Factory). Hitachi Construction Machinery Co., Ltd. China Factory also Located in Hefei.

The company was established in 1995. Hefei Panri Construction Machinery Trading Co., Ltd. started from the main business of Heming brand WY60, WY80 excavator accessories and modified Russian excavators. Now it has developed more brand excavator accessories business, Sany, Carter, Komatsu, Hyundai, Doosan, and other excavator parts and oil maintenance parts.

In 2022, for better development, the company applied for import and export certification to open up overseas markets for global customers. Since 2002, after 20 years of development, the company has a certain scale and strength, and has achieved an annual turnover of 110 million yuan, and has also accumulated rich professional experience and a good reputation in the industry.
