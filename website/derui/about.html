Anhui Dery Material Science Co., Ltd.

Established in 2006, Anhui Dery Material Science Co., Ltd. (formerly Hongkong Carbon Chemical Co., Ltd.) is an innovative industrial manufacturing enterprise integrating R&D and production, sales and technical support of special pigment carbon black. Based on demand of domestic and foreign market and niche industry positioning, Dery actively explores surface oxidation-treatment technology of carbon black and achieves technical breakthrough and products quality improvement steadily with years of accumulation. Widely used in fields like high-end coatings, inks, plastics, sealants,leather color paste, fibers coloring, textile printing color paste, Oxidized Deryblack series are fairly popular among customers at home and abroad and share good markets in more than 50 countries and regions like Korea, Japan, Taiwan, Hongkong, India, Thailand, Pakistan, Turkey, Russia, Germany, Kenya, Egypt and so on.

 

As a national high-tech. enterprise, Dery invested and built a professional production base conforming to international standards in Guangde, Anhui Province, an advantageous area in the Yangtze River Delta. Now Dery owns 2 production lines with annual output of 5,000tons general carbon black and 6 sets oxidation equipment with annual output of 6000tons oxidized high-end carbon black. In recent years, Dery founded high-end water-based black color paste production plant with annual output 4000 tons to strengthen and extend supporting industries on original basis.

 

Sticking to the corporation vision of Embracing the world and Creating the future, Dery people will continue cultivating the special carbon black industry and strive to be leading solutions provider of engineer carbon black application-system in China and even the world.
