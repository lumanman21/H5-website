    about Hefei Jingweite Electronics Co., Ltd.
    • Hefei Jingweite Electronics Co., Ltd. is located in the national economic and technological development zone of Hefei City. It is a national high-tech enterprise integrating R&D, production and sales of crystal frequency devices. After more than 20 years of unremitting efforts and struggles, the scale and innovation ability of the enterprise and its comprehensive strength ranks at the forefront of the domestic industry.
