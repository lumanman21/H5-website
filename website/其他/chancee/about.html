ABOUT US

about chancee

Committed to becoming the world's leading and trusted cleaning equipment provider
With industrial and commercial cleaning equipment as the core, it integrates independent research and development, manufacturing, sales and service, and has provided 460000 sets of various cleaning equipment. It is an ODM (original design manufacturer) factory of many famous brands such as Yangzi, Royalstar, and is the "invisible champion" in the domestic cleaning equipment industry.

With years of experience in cleaning equipment technology accumulation and manufacturing, we have reached strategic cooperation with technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China, and launched the research and development of a new generation of cleaning equipment for Industry 4.0. The overall quality has increased by 50% - 150% on average compared with the previous generation of products. More than 100 patents have been obtained and are being applied for, and we have passed the dual quality certification of Swiss SGS and China Saibao Laboratory. It has four production bases in Hefei, Nantong, Chuzhou and Dingyuan, covering an area of more than 500 mu, covering dozens of categories such as "floor scubbers, floor sweepers, vacuum cleaners, high-pressure cleaners, cleaning tools and detergents", more than 1000 products, 50000 square distribution storage centers, and directly reaching the national logistics distribution. It is one of the cleaning equipment manufacturers with the largest sales in China.

development history

2005

Pioneering

The founders' team began to venture into the field of cleaning equipment.

2016

Breaking

Set up a cleaning equipment company in Chuzhou and introduce German standard production lines.

2017

Gathering Situation

Yangzi Group authorizes the establishment of Yangzi Industrial Technology, which is fully responsible for the R&D, manufacturing, sales and operation of Yangzi brand cleaning equipment, ranking first in online sales.

2018

Forethought

Relying on years of R&D and manufacturing experience, we have successively reached strategic cooperation with technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China to benchmark international cutting-edge products, and launched the research and development of a new generation of cleaning equipment code named "CHANCEE", with the overall quality improved by 50% - 150% compared with the previous generation.

2020

Innovation

The new manufacturing base in Hefei has been completed. Together with technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China, it has built a research triangle with the CHANCEE Industrial Laboratory.

2021

Take Off

New factory of CHANCEE was completed in Hefei. Together with the technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China, and CHANCEE Industrial Laboratory, it has built an intelligent research triangle. More than 100 patents have been obtained and are being applied for.

factory environment

research centers

CHANCEE self built CHANCEE industrial laboratory and CHANCEE MRO innovation research institute are committed to product development and intelligent manufacturing in the field of industrial and commercial cleaning, making due contributions to human cleaning.

We have successively reached strategic cooperation with technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China to jointly develop projects such as the Development and Application of Intelligent System for Cleaning Equipment, and the Intelligent Transformation Scheme for Small and Medium sized Industrial and Commercial Cleaning Equipment for Indoor and Outdoor Application Scenarios, providing core technical support for the third generation of CHANCEE products, so that the intelligence, automation, and humanization of CHANCEE products always take the lead in the industry.

four bases

CHANCEE has been focusing on the manufacturing of cleaning equipment for 16 years. It has four production bases, covering an area of more than 500 mu. It has many German standard production lines, covering dozens of categories such as "floor washers, sweepers, vacuum cleaners, high-pressure washers, cleaning tools", more than 1000 products, 50000 square distribution storage center, and direct logistics distribution throughout the country. It is one of the largest cleaning equipment manufacturers in China.
