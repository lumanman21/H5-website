Who is CHANCEE

Committed to becoming the world's leading and trusted cleaning equipment provider
With industrial and commercial cleaning equipment as the core, it integrates independent research and development, manufacturing, sales and service, and has provided 460000 sets of various cleaning equipment. It is an ODM (original design manufacturer) factory of many famous brands such as Yangzi, Royalstar, and is the "invisible champion" in the domestic cleaning equipment industry.

With years of experience in cleaning equipment technology accumulation and manufacturing, we have reached strategic cooperation with technical experts from the Chinese Academy of Sciences and the University of Science and Technology of China, and launched the research and development of a new generation of cleaning equipment for Industry 4.0. The overall quality has increased by 50% - 150% on average compared with the previous generation of products. More than 100 patents have been obtained and are being applied for, and we have passed the dual quality certification of Swiss SGS and China Saibao Laboratory.
