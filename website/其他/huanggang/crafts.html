  • Industry status quo

    In recent years, the export of bamboo and grass products has been growing rapidly, and the product types have been innovating constantly. It has developed from bamboo, rattan, willow and straw crafts to wooden crafts, iron products and mixed products with higher added value. The products involve craft products, small furniture, kitchen utensils, gardening products and festival goods. It covers more than 100 countries in the world, including America, Europe, Asia and Africa.

    Special bamboo and wooden products won orders. In the 5th Forest Expo, bamboo bicycles, bamboo furniture, bamboo crafts, bamboo daily necessities, wood structure wood architecture, flowers and horticulture... The products have their own characteristics, so that people are overwhelmed. It is a very practical topic how to use the effective method and experience of the bamboo grass product overseas marketing, develop our country enterprise's bamboo grass product export.

  • Welcome to join hands with Ahhuanggang (ANHUI HUANGGANG LIUBIAN CRAFT CO.,LTD. ) to develop the market.

    We will explore emerging markets and expand international cooperation networks. Our country should formulate and implement the diversification measures of bamboo and grass products export market, establish the public information service system of foreign economic and trade, and provide free international information for enterprises to expand the market. In addition to the three major markets of Europe, the United States and Australia, we should actively explore emerging markets, such as the Middle East, Latin America and Eastern Europe. India and some countries and regions in Southeast Asia have developed rapidly in recent years, with a large population and a large demand market. In addition, the National Forestry Administration and other relevant institutions can establish a database of green forest products enterprises, timely release the information of green forest products that enterprises can provide, and expand the international cooperation network, so as to prevent the market from being affected by the policies of importing countries or regions and technical trade measures. In addition, according to the different characteristics and needs of the export market, the product structure can be adjusted, the product varieties can be diversified, and the export characteristics and advantages of bamboo, wood and grass products can be improved.
