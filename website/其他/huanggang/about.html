about Ahhuanggang

ANHUI HUANGGANG LIUBIAN CRAFT CO.,LTD.

ANHUI HUANGGANG LIUBIAN CRAFT CO.,LTD. was established in 2020. The company is located in the industrial zone of Zhonggang Town, Funan County, Anhui Province - the famous willow town in China, at the junction of S328 and Fucao Road, and is 40km away from Fuyang Airport and Fuyang High-speed Railway. It takes 20 minutes to reach the exit of Jiguang Expressway, enjoying a convenient traffic. The company covers an area of 8666 square meters, and the building area is more than 5000 square meters. The fixed assets is 12 million yuan and the working capital is 7 million yuan.

The main products of ANHUI HUANGGANG LIUBIAN CRAFT CO.,LTD. are Furniture, willow products, wooden products and grass products. The company possesses a large number of relatively stable customer groups. It integrates professional design, production, sales and export, possesses a sound management system and a perfect and effective overall quality management system, and has a fast-responsive and thoughtful after-sales service system. It also has a professional technical team which keeps research & development and innovation. The company now has 12 enterprise management persons, 6 product designers, 20 marketing persons, 36 model weaving persons, 26 delivery team leaders, driving 6000 close farmer knitters. At the same time, the company adopts a production and business operation mode of company + base + farmers.

High-tech equipment, high-level management, and high-quality workforce are the foundation for rapid development of the company. As a company with innovative 5S and lean production management, based on the concept of "we exist for customers", we will, as always, take "development with innovation, survival with quality, and customer satisfaction" as our aim, and serve the customers wholeheartedly .

The main products of Anhui Hongrun Manufacturing Co.,ltd are traditional handicrafts, willow, wood and straw products of local farmers. The company possesses a large number of relatively stable customer groups. It integrates professional design, production, sales and export, possesses a sound management system and a perfect and effective overall quality management system, and has a fast-responsive and thoughtful after-sales service system. It also has a professional technical team which keeps research & development and innovation. The company now has 12 enterprise management persons, 6 product designers, 20 marketing persons, 36 model weaving persons, 26 delivery team leaders, driving 6000 close farmer knitters. At the same time, the company adopts a production and business operation mode of company + base + farmers. In 2019, the production scale will be increased. It is expected to earn foreign exchange through exports (6.5 million US dollars), which will promote the rapid development of the local economy, so that the farmers can quickly get rid of poverty and become rich.

Honorary certificate title

The company has set offices in Shanghai and Ningbo, and employed professionals to carry out marketing work to explore the international market. It has won the trust of domestic and foreign customers at the exhibition of the two annual Canton Fairs. It now has fixed domestic and foreign customers, and its products are exported to more than ten countries and regions such as Netherlands, Germany, the United States, Canada, and Japan.
