The application of scientific knowledge for practical purposes,

Time of Release : 2023-02-20

Detailed introduction

Huangshan KBR New Material Technology Co., Ltd.  silane coupling agents cover  sulfur type of silane, amino silane, vinyl silane, epoxy silane, alkyl silane and phenyl    silane, methyl acryloyl oxygen radicals silane, silane crosslinking agent and silane coupling agent intermediates 9types,more than 70 kinds of products.Product are widely used in paint coatings, printing ink, rubber products, adhesives and tires, modified plastics, textile printing and dyeing, oil field industry, casting, and other industrial products manufacturing and so on more than 50 industries. Silane coupling agents chemical department currently is running 70 types products. Mainly depends on the development for organic silicon in huangshan and application of multi-functional pilot production workshop, laboratory technical support and application development support,Put the Shandong dongying stock cooperation of organic silicon new material factory product sales in the frist place. Silicone new material factory has a set of production line with capacity 25000mt/year for  trichlorosilane, Chlorine propylene production line with capacity 25000mt/year,16 sets of γ-chloropropylated silane production equipments with capacity 10000mt./year,4 sets of production equipments with capacity 8000mt/year for γ2（γ-Chloropropyl Triethoxysilane and γ-Chloropropyltrimethoxysilane）(4 production lines for γ2). More than 5000mt Silane coupling agents  were producted by the use of  γ2 intermediates. Factory in strictly regulated by the government supervision and administration of production safety and environmental protection bureau. After years of reform and upgrade. All the devices, raw material storage area, product loading area have realized DCS control and safety dynamic detection, has set up complete  off-gas treatment system, production wastewater treatment systems and by-products deep processing equipment; has got ISO9000 and ISO14000 system certificate.
