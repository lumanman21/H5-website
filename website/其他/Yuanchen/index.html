ABOUT US

Guardian of
Yuanchen's global
ecological environment

Yuanchen Technology was founded in 2005, development has not changed the original intention. Facing the major national needs, focus on environmental protection technology research and development and application, in the industrial flue gas dust removal, denitrification, circular economy, intelligent environmental protection and other fields of innovation; It is committed to leading the technological frontier of the industry and promoting the integration of new business forms in the environmental protection industry. It is the only enterprise in China with the qualification of R&D, design and production of high efficiency dust removal filter materials and SCR denitrification catalyst as well as recycling and recycling of waste catalyst. It has the qualification of CNAS and CMA testing.
