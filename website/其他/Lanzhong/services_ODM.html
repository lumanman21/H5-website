ODM service

As insist on high quality, Lanzhong has established long-term OEM and ODM cooperation with over 90% Chinese fire water mist system manufacturers and some overseas system manufacturers.

Lanzhong also has rich experience in other fire nozzles, industrial nozzles, cleaning nozzles, agricultural nozzles and etc.

In the environment of cheap labor costs in China, Lanzhong is able to provide our clients with economical, fast and reliable OEM services with its rich experience in nozzle design and production, complete production testing equipment, and scientific quality management system.
