How Lanzhong is different
from other nozzle manufacturers?

  • 01

    Keep Pursuing High Quality

    Lanzhong originated from fire-fighting water mist nozzles with high quality requirements, having formed our high quality standard. Integrating the standard into the production of other types of nozzles, Lanzhong has built our brand of high-quality nozzles!

  • 02

    Excellent Nozzle Design Ability

    Lanzhong serves most of our clients by providing nozzle design and production services according to customer needs and application. Therefore, Lanzhong has accumulated rich experience in nozzles design and application, can provide fast and reliable solutions for different customers.

  • 03

    Scientific Quality Management System

    Lanzhong adopts process quality control and 100% testing of key parameters of finished products to ensure the ultra-high pass rate of products, forming our scientific and efficient QC system.

  • 04

    Service System And Fast Delivery

    In the process of serving many domestic and foreign customers from different industries, Lanzhong has established a standardized and efficient service system to ensure rapid response to customer needs and fast delivery.

  • 05

    Small Sample Orders Are Welcome

    Some customers cannot find a good supplier due to the need for customization and the small amount of orders. It is by serving many such clients that Lanzhong has grown; we are grateful for the support of the customers, so Lanzhong always welcomes small quantity sample orders.
