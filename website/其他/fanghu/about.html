Tianchang Dongan Protective Equipment Co., Ltd. Locates in Tianchang city,founded in 1997.It is a company mainly selling plush toys, We have over 300 workers , more than 4000 square meters 100000 degree purification workshop and more than 150 square meters 100000 degree laboratory.

Our main products are: plush toys,teddy bears,keychain,home decor plush,customized plush toys,pet toys and so on. We have a number of cooperative factories in China. They all have years of experience in designing and developing plush toys related products. The plush toys related supply chains are complete, and the quality of the products is good.The production line runs mature and reliable.

We have own brand and good product market feedback as well. We can accept customized plush toys and their fabrics, fillers, logos, labels, packaging, etc. according to your preferences and needs.Our company has always adhered to the basic principles of reasonable prices, high-quality products, punctual delivery, and good reputation services. All products adopt international quality standards, and the products are exported to overseas, and are well-known in many markets at our country and abroad, and have received a lot of praises.

Our company will do its best to provide customers with better and higher quality services and give customers the best shopping experience. Tianchang Dongan Protective Equipment Co.,Ltd. will continue to advance and become a lieutenant general in the international market.
