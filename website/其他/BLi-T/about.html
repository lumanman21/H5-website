    About Us

    Better Chemical,Better Life

    BLi-T (Hefei) Chemical Co., Ltd.

    Learn More
    BLi-T (Hefei) Chemical Co., Ltd.is a professional enterprise integrating production, research and development,and trade.We established in 2017,and been committed to providing high-quality chemicals to customers at competitive prices and offering customized services for some products.

    We have our own factory ,testing and R&D Center,and with years of industry experience, we have established long-term partnerships with many reputable manufacturers and suppliers, it enabling us to offer a wide range of chemical products, including fine chemicals, solvents, cosmetics, food additives, water treatment chemicals, agricultural chemicals, etc.

    In addition, we have strong capabilities in cosmetic customization services,and providing different solutions and formulation support for cosmetic companies. Our technicians can also provide help and advice in the field of chemicals.

    At BLi-T Chemical, we adhere to the motto "Better chemical, better life," committed to providing customers with high-quality services and products that are suitable for future development. Our team is always ready to help you solve your chemical needs or problems.
