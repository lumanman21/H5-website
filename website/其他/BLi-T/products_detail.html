    D-Panthenol

    Description

    D-panthenol is a colorless to slightly yellow transparent viscous liquid. It may crystallize if stored at low temperatures for a long time. It has a slight special odor and is hygroscopic. It is widely used in the pharmaceutical, food, and cosmetic industries.
    • CAS:38083-17-9
    • Synonyms:Panthenol,Dexpanthenol
    • EINECS:201-327-3
    • Molecular Formula:C9H19NO4
    • level:USP/EP
    • Package:20kg/drum or according to customer requirements
