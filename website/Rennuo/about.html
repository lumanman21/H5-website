About Rennuo
Anhui Longshun is a company with 2 manufacture sites,one in China,the other in Korea.Our Korean team,Careline Corp,has been professional in scooter/power wheelchair business for over 30 years,supplying finished products to Pride,Invacare,Sunrise Medical &etc up to late 2000. With more than 30 year experience in scooters and power wheelchair,we have taken almost 80% market share in S-Korean market,in qty around 15,000 units a year. Longshun is not only a factory for mobility scooters and power wheelchair,but also a manufacturer for its related components,like scooter seat,frame,bumpers,covers etc.Longshun has been offering scooter components,like scooter seat,bumpers and related steel part,to international brand,like Afikim & Pride.
