A focused technology company

Anhui Mascotop Electronic Co., Ltd. is a high-tech enterprise specializing in the development of capacitors. The company was founded in 2006, with a registered capital of 10 million yuan. It is located in Heli Park, Ningguo Economic and Technological Development Zone. The company is committed to the research and development, production and sales of metallized film capacitors and high voltage capacitors for microwave ovens.

Over the years, Mascotop has been recognized by customers all over the world for its competitive advantages such as technology research and development close to the market, first-class quality and service, fast and flexible response speed. It provides film capacitor "one-stop" solutions for various complete machine customers, such as household appliances, lighting, communication, industrial control, automotive electronics, green energy, etc., and its products are sold all over the country and exported to Europe and North America Asia, South America, the Middle East, Southeast Asia, Africa and other regions around the world have become the main suppliers of leading enterprises in various fields, and are widely trusted and praised by all walks of life. Invite customers from all over the world, reputation comes from quality. In the future development, Mascotop will, as always, take advanced equipment and advanced technology as the foundation, adhere to scientific and technological innovation, shape product quality with scientific management, and continue to cooperate with domestic and foreign merchants with sincere cooperation and after-sales management experience honed by enterprises in market competition for many years!

After years of development and innovation, Mascotop has always been at the forefront of the industry's scientific and technological development, and led the industry to move forward in a new direction. As a result of his outstanding achievements in the film capacitor industry, Mr. Wang Jun, chairman of the board of Mascotop company, was entrusted by the National Technical Committee for standardization of lighting appliances to draft two national standards, i.e. "capacitors for tubular fluorescent lamps and other discharge lamp lines - General requirements and safety requirements" and "capacitors for tubular fluorescent lamps and other discharge lamp circuits - Performance requirements". The company has established good cooperative relations with Xi'an University of Electronic Science and technology, Shanghai Jiaotong University, Beijing electric light source Research Institute, Anhui material engineering school and other scientific research institutions, and has become the summer internship base of Xi'an University of Electronic Science and technology and Hefei University of technology.

Good reputation
